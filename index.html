Who We Are

Empowering Families, Transforming Lives

Acmedics Development Centre exists to bridge the gap between clinical intervention and everyday home life. We believe that a child's progress accelerates when parents are equipped with professional knowledge, practical tools, and a caring, evidence-based support network.

Whether online or in-person, our programs are designed to accommodate your family's dynamic while maintaining scientific rigor and compassionate engagement.

Our Mission

To equip families with the knowledge, tools, and support they need to help their children thrive at home, in school, and in everyday life.

Our Vision

A world where children with developmental differences are fully understood, supported, and celebrated for their unique strengths and contributions.

Meet the Founder

Happiness Oredipe

Educator, Child Development Coach, and Mother

Happiness Oredipe is an experienced educator and child development coach with over 15 years of professional dedication to early child education and special needs support.

Beyond her professional qualifications, Happiness's deepest drive is personal. As a mother of four, including children raised with autism and speech delay, she understands the emotional hurdles, diagnostic fears, and triumphs that parents go through every single day.

She has supported families across multiple countries, developing practical, evidence-based tools that parents can execute without feeling overwhelmed.

"My personal journey raising children with autism taught me that parents are a child's most impactful therapists. At Acmedics, we don't just treat children; we empower and coach parents to make everyday routines therapeutic."

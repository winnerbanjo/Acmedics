Our Story

Empowering Parents, Unlocking Potential

Acmedics Development Centre bridges the gap between clinical child intervention and home parenting routines, worldwide.

15+ Years Experience
Happiness Oredipe

A Mother's Devotion, A Coach's Expertise

Happiness Oredipe is a dedicated special needs educator, child development coach, and the founder of Acmedics Development Centre. Over the past 15 years, she has supported educational institutions, NGOs, and hundreds of families navigating developmental delays, speech difficulties, autism, and ADHD.

But her primary credential comes from home. As a mother of four, including children raised with autism and speech delays, she knows the raw emotions, the confusion, and the constant adjustments that parents face.

She has committed her life to making evidence-based therapy methods accessible, practical, and highly integrated into parent-child interaction at home.

Our Operations

Physical Presence & Online Global Reach

Acmedics Development Centre operates locally out of Lagos, Nigeria, providing in-person speech support, cognitive assessments, and classroom shadow training within primary and secondary educational systems.

For families abroad, our virtual assessments, online parenting workshops, digital milestones, and parent coaching programs connect families in the UK, US, Canada, UAE, Ireland, and across Africa to professional assistance.
